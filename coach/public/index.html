NFHS Profile

Who am I?

I am Luke Powers of California, born and raised. I have been coaching with AYSO since 2017, having run an All Star team each year as well. I am an avid soccer/football fan, and can be found either playing (midfielder), or watching (Go Man United!) the beautiful game.

My Philosophy is to help players develop appropriate soccer TEAM skills and provide chances for players to work towards achieving personal development goals in soccer. To this end, the skills of passing, shooting and off the ball movement are emphasized over nutmegs and fancy flicks. Players are expected to be aggressive and constantly striving to make a difference in the game.
